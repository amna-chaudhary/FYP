What the System Delivers

Four pillars of the implementation

An intelligent decentralized system for Granular Energy Certificates — uniting agentic AI, blockchain-backed ownership, and peer-to-peer trading into one cohesive academic software solution.

01
On-chain certificate lifecycle

Full issuance, transfer, retirement, and verification backed by Move smart contracts — every action logged as an auditable Aptos transaction.

02
Decentralized marketplace

Peer-to-peer GEC trading with transparent pricing, live supply-vs-demand data, and on-chain settlement — no centralized broker in between.

03
Blockchain-backed identity

Each user controls their certificates through their own Aptos account — cryptographic ownership with no central authority or permission hierarchy.

04
Agentic natural-language control

An LLM chatbot with RAG and MCP turns plain-English commands into verified registry and marketplace actions — no technical commands required.

System Modules

Three connected parts of the platform

The platform is decomposed into three core modules — a blockchain-backed registry, a decentralized trading marketplace, and an agentic AI assistant — that together form a complete GEC management system.

Registry Module

Certificate lifecycle operations powered by Move smart contracts on Aptos — every issue, transfer, and retirement is an auditable on-chain event.

  • Issue EnergyTag-compliant granular energy certificates
  • Transfer ownership between Aptos blockchain accounts
  • Retire certificates with complete transaction history
  • Inspect balances, tx hashes, and metadata in one view
Marketplace Module

A decentralized exchange (DEX) for peer-to-peer certificate trading with live session data, transparent pricing, and on-chain settlement.

  • Browse live buy-side and sell-side opportunities
  • Visualize supply, demand, and price bands by technology
  • Match buyers and sellers without centralized brokers
  • Settle trades directly through blockchain transactions
Agentic AI Assistant

A natural-language chatbot that interprets user intent and routes it to the correct registry or marketplace action — powered by LLMs, RAG, and the Model Context Protocol.

  • Issue, transfer, retire via plain-English commands
  • RAG pipeline for domain-accurate, grounded responses
  • MCP servers bridge the AI to blockchain tools
  • Fallback validation handles ambiguous prompts safely
How It Works

From natural language to the blockchain

The agentic workflow turns a single sentence from the user into a verified on-chain certificate operation — no CLI, no raw API calls, no blockchain literacy required.

1

Authenticate & enter

Sign in securely and land in the assistant workspace — your Aptos account is bound to every action you take from here.

2

Ask in plain language

Type commands like “issue 5 solar to account 1 in Lahore” or “show live marketplace”. The chatbot parses intent through an LLM + RAG pipeline.

3

Agent executes on-chain

The AI agent maps intent to Move smart-contract calls via MCP, signs the transaction, and commits it to the Aptos blockchain.

4

Verify & trade

Results appear instantly in the registry — balances, tx hashes, audit log — and any excess can be listed on the decentralized marketplace.

Project Scope

Scope, stack, and academic context

The boundaries, technology choices, and academic framing of the system as submitted for the Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering programme.

Project objective

Transforming Energy Certificate Markets via Agentic Computing and Decentralized Market Mechanisms. The system digitalizes the full GEC lifecycle — issuance, verification, transfer, retirement, and peer-to-peer trading — using an agentic AI interface on top of Aptos blockchain. It integrates EnergyTag-compliant certificate structures, Move smart contract APIs, and the Model Context Protocol to automate operations through natural-language commands, replacing manual, centralized, and opaque workflows with transparent on-chain trust.

Aptos Blockchain Move Smart Contracts LLM + RAG Model Context Protocol EnergyTag Decentralized DEX

Academic context

Final Year Project submitted to the Department of Software Engineering, University of Gujrat, for the degree of Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering (BSSE 2022–2026). Supervised by Mr. Adeel Shahzad, the project demonstrates an end-to-end agentic system from requirements analysis through on-chain deployment.
